A gourmet experience

With over 6 lakh sq. ft. of retail space to explore, we at Select SHOPPING MALL make sure there is something for everyone. The extensive range of international chains and numerous interesting restaurants will give you an option to choose how you want to indulge your taste buds.

From having fun with friends over a cup of coffee at Starbucks or The Coffee Bean to sophisticated dinners and cocktails at Burma Burma or Pa Pa Ya, the shopping centre ensures that you won’t stay hungry for long! Our selection of confectioners and patisseries such as L’Opera and Theobroma, Ice Cream and frozen yogurt parlor such as Red Mango, make sure that dessert lovers are spoilt for choice.

At Shopping Mall, every dining experience is unique and exquisite. With an extensive range of ‘tastefully selected’ restaurants, the most appetizing food, and a number of cuisines from around the world, we create an experience that’ll leave your taste buds happy every time!
